Good Days - SZA

About the Song

"Good Days" is a song by American singer SZA. Released in December 2020, the song explores themes of self-love, growth, and overcoming obstacles. It received critical acclaim for its dreamy production and introspective lyrics.

About the Artist

Solána Imani Rowe, known professionally as SZA, is an American singer-songwriter. Born on November 8, 1989, in St. Louis, Missouri, SZA rose to prominence with her debut studio album "Ctrl" in 2017, which earned her multiple Grammy nominations. Known for her soulful voice and honest songwriting, SZA has become one of the leading voices in contemporary R&B.
